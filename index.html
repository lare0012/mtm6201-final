Why Choose
Cats of Ottawa?

At Ottawa Cats, we do more than just re-home we rescue, rehabilitate, and build lasting bonds between cats and caring humans. Each of our cats is nurtured in a safe environment, health-checked, and matched thoughtfully to the right family. We're local, compassionate, and deeply committed to giving every cat in Ottawa–Gatineau a second chance at life and love.

How It Works?

Adopting with us is simple and stress-free. Start by browsing our adorable cats online, then fill out a quick adoption form. Once we review your application, we'll schedule a meet-and-greet to help you connect with the right cat. If it's a match, we'll guide you through the final steps so you can welcome your new furry friend home in no time.
